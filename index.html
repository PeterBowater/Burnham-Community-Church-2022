Burnham Community Church

Where you can get a fresh start



Welcome to Burnham Community Church!

Our aim is to show the love of God to those we meet & to serve the community in which we live.

If you’re looking for a church to belong to or your simply visiting while on holiday, you are always welcome to join us on Sundays for worship and fellowship or at any of our weekly meetings or events.

What we do

location_on Where & when we meet

We meet every Sunday at Highbridge Community Hall from 10:15am for worship and fellowship.

Park in Bank Street car park (free!) and come in the double doors at the back of the building.

festival What happens?

Our services are family-friendly, lively and informal, anyone of any age is welcome!

We fellowship, worship, have bible-based teaching and kids activities.

food_bank What else?

We meet outside of our regular Sunday service, eat together, talk about life and the bible and learn from one another.

Our goal is build a real community of people who are passionate about the Lord and the church.

volunteer_activism Need help?

We all have difficult times in our lives, but whatever they may be, we can help you, pray with you, and walk with you through to the end.

We love to help.

More about Burnham Community Church

Sermons

We record all our sermons, and share them over on our Facebook page.

Connect groups

We have a number of groups that regularly meet in our members' homes, we eat, pray and learn about eachother and God.

Prayer

We meet in 'The Boiler Room' every Sunday before our service for prayer, we pray for the church, community, nations and leaders.

Every other Saturday we 'Rise and Pray' at a member's home from 7:30am.

Community Events

We love to run events purely for the community, our most popular one our open air Carols by Candlelight in Manor Gardens which is held the Sunday before Christmas.

See some great photos from previous years over on our Facebook page.


For more details on anything, please get in touch with us, or speak to one of our members when you visit on a Sunday.
